## Form Integration - Google Sheets

### How Forms Work:

Both "Request Quote" and "Contact" forms automatically submit data to your Google Sheets spreadsheet:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1gvQCOBoUjV7GOB-YNLxP9EzRnfG2rMGsjL25goU81Xw/

**Data Fields Captured:**
- Name, Phone, Email
- Service Type
- Location/Description
- Message/Budget
- Timestamp (with IST timezone)

**After Form Submission:**
1. Data saves to Google Sheets automatically
2. WhatsApp notification is sent to you
3. User gets success confirmation
